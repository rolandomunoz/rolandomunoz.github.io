Welcome to my personal website. Here, you will find some tools I have designed that are useful when working in linguistic fieldwork.
Since I am closer to the Phonetics and Phonology fields, so are these tools. However, some of them can be used with other purposes.

I also have included a section called Spanish Vowels, where you will find some phonetic materials I have collected with help of various speakers of Spanish from Lima (Peru).

As this website is still under construction, some parts of it are not available yet. Alternatively, you can visit my other projects on GitHub and GitLab.

All suggestions and contributions are welcomed. Please, feel free to contact me.
